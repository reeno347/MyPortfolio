Polirevo - Basketball Robot

The function of Robot 1st:

  • Dribbling the ball
  • Shooting the ball.

    • Robot 1st Mechanism:

    The dribble mechanism is pushed using a pneumatic cylinder for the ball to bounce on the floor. The ball is pushed from above using a pneumatic cylinder at the same time as the ball holder is opened which is moved by the pneumatic cylinder. After the ball bounces, the ball holder cylinder pushes and holds the ball back on the dribble mechanism. The ball is pulled back using a pneumatic cylinder. When the dribble mechanism is in the back position, the ball is dropped into the robot and the ball will roll to the throwing mechanism. The ball will stop rolling by the holder on the mechanism, the ball is thrown by turning on two DC motors, and when the motor rotation is in accordance with the target, the ball will be raised to the spinning wheel using a pneumatic cylinder. The ball that is thrown or thrown can be directed towards robot 2 or the basketball hoop.

    The function of Robot 2nd :

  • Catch the ball
  • Blocking the ball shot by the opponent
  • Height adjustment of the catcher with pulley and belt system.
  • Firing the ball

    • Robot 2nd Mechanism :

    The robot's up and down system is used to receive the ball from robot 1 or block the opponent's shot or blocking the ball shot from the opponent. To adjust the height of the ball catch or block the opponent's ball shot, the robot uses an up and down mechanism made from V-Slot Profile Aluminum with wheels and a pulley and timing belt lifting system. After the ball is received, the ball will roll until it is stopped by the holder. Just like in the ejection system of robot 1, this robot shoots the ball by turning on the DC motor that drives the wheels. The ball by starting the DC motor that drives the ejection wheel. When the rotation of the ejection wheel has reached the desired speed, the ball is lifted towards the ejector using a pneumatic cylinder system. The fired or thrown ball is directed towards the ball ring.
